Company Overview

Clients expect accurate, efficient and economical measured survey advice. Light Check delivers, coupled with two decades of experience servicing design practitioners in design related disciplines - most notably the architectural, landscaping, planning and interior design specialist fields. To assist with budgetary and timing contraints, deliverables are available on a sliding detail scale, from relatively basic layout drawings through to highly specified heritage asset records.

Our most common measured survey deliverables:

  • Floor Plans
  • Area Caculations
  • Internal and External Elevations
  • Topographical Plans
  • Location Plans
  • Cross-Sections
  • Co-ordinating Verified View Information
  • 3D Solid Digital Modelling
  • As-built checks and records
